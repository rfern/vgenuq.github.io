Events



March

Monday 17th - Screening of "12 Years a Slave"(7:30 PM - 10:00 PM)

Schonell Theatre at UQ is holding a screening of the Academy and Oscar awards winning film 12 Years a Slave. This is a great event for VGenners to get together to watch a film about a passion close to all of our hearts: learning about and fighting social injustice. This is an early event in regards to International Day of Remembrance of Slaves on the 25th of March. This event will also bring together social activists from around uni, which makes it a great opportunity to meet and talk with other like-minded people who share a similar World Vision (sorry not sorry ;) ). We may all be a part of different groups, but our aim - a world free from injustice - is shared!

Event open to EVERYONE!!!

TICKETS: $6 for UQers, $8 - everyone else with concession card and $12 adult! if we can 30 people to RSVP that means more discounts for everyone!


Tuesday 11th - Picnic in the Great Court (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

We’ll be setting up afternoon tea in the Great Court as an opportunity for you to meet your executive team for 2014. There will be food and drinks provided so come along and join us for a relaxed afternoon and take advantage of this chance to meet some amazing new friends who share the same passion for social justice and the alleviation of poverty.


Tuesday 4th - BBQ @ Cooper Rd Roundabout (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM)

Come join us as the exec team cooks up a storm! Halal meat, limited vegetarian options and drinks will all be available!



February

Wednesday 26th - Market Day

VGen UQ will be out in force on Market Day. We will be set up just near the Michie Building with the other social justice clubs, and we can’t wait to see you there! Don’t forget to sign up for membership, enter our raffle and pick up some freebies while you’re at it!


For further information about our events please like us on facebook.
