1. Start of 2014:

After an amazing year last year, the VGen UQ team are SUPER excited to hit the ground running in 2014. We’re ready to bring you even more events and even more opportunities throughout the year to empower you to take action on issues of social injustice. Be sure to come visit our stall on Market Day and sign up to what we think is the best club at UQ. We’ll be based just by the Michie Building (look for the orange!) and we are kicking off the year with a raffle (prize includes some awesome VGen goodies, a Boost Juice Voucher and more!) and pick up some freebies while you’re at it! Hope to see you there!!

2. Donation to Child Rescue:

Better late than never, right? On Sunday 23rd February, VGen UQ donated over $500 to World Vision Australia’s Child Rescue program. This money was raised throughout 2013 on campus and at our Charity Cocktail Night held in partnership with UQ Oaktree. Such a tremendous donation would not have been possible without the help and support of our incredible members, so we thank you all for the support you have shown us and we hope to repay you with even more incredible opportunities throughout the year to come. Child Rescue is an incredible program designed to help children who are outside the reach of World Vision’s regular Child Sponsorship programs. Children who, for whatever reason, are at higher risk of exploitation and trafficking. Through Child Rescue, World Vision is helping these children to regain their childhood and achieve their potential. For more information, go to www.worldvision.com.au/childrescue
